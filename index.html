About Us

The MAN Group's odyssey began in 2022 in the heart of Virginia's bustling business district. With a singular vision to revolutionize the restaurant industry through the power of analytics and data-driven decision-making, our founders – a group of like-minded data scientists, marketing gurus, and culinary aficionados – laid the foundation of what would become a beacon of innovation in the restaurant consultancy arena.

From our humble beginnings in a small, sunlit office where ideas flew as rapidly as the coding keystrokes, the MAN Group has grown into a bastion of strategic prowess, boasting a clientele that spans the breadth of the gastronomy landscape, from quaint bistros to international restaurant chains.
