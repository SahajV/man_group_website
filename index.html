About Us

Compassion in Action: Our Journey
Discover our story at Animal Welfare Society, a haven for neglected and abandoned animals. Since 2023, our dedicated team has been rescuing, rehabilitating, and providing a loving temporary home for these animals. We believe every creature deserves a chance at happiness and work tirelessly to make this a reality. Join us in our mission to create a kinder world for our furry friends.

Virtual Adoption Program

Find Your Forever Friend from Home
Embrace the joy of pet parenthood through our Virtual Adoption Program. Browse our online gallery to meet our adorable residents waiting for a loving home. Our virtual adoption process is seamless, ensuring you find the perfect match. Hear heartwarming stories of families united with their pets through our program and become part of this beautiful journey.

Awareness and Education

Empower Through Education: Our Awareness Campaigns
Dive into our Awareness and Education programs designed to foster a community of informed and responsible pet owners. From understanding pet needs to recognizing the importance of spaying/neutering, we offer valuable resources for all. Participate in our workshops, events, and volunteer programs to spread the message of animal welfare far and wide.
